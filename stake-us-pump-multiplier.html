Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the Pump Game on Stake.us?

The Pump Game is a crash-style betting game where the multiplier increases over time. Players must cash out before the pump bursts to secure their winnings.

What is the highest multiplier in Pump Game?

The highest possible multiplier is 3,203,384.80x in Expert mode.

Is there a strategy to win in Pump Game?

Many players use strategies like early cash-outs, progressive betting, and analyzing past trends to maximize their success.
